# Privacy Policy for One Line Habit

**Last Updated:** December 26, 2025

## Introduction

One Line Habit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

One Line Habit operates entirely offline. All your habit data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to, collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information or habit data.

## Data Storage

- All data is stored **locally on your device only**
- Data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any server
- We cannot access, view, or retrieve your data
- No data is synced to iCloud or any cloud service

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data.

## Third-Party Services

One Line Habit does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not use:
- Analytics tools
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting services
- User tracking technologies

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:
- **Microphone**: Used only for voice input to add habits. Audio is processed locally and never recorded or stored.
- **Speech Recognition**: Used only to convert your voice to text. Processing happens entirely on your device.

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply delete the App from your device. All habit data will be permanently removed.

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App or updating this page.
